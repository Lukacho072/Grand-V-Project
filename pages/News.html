Holiday Celebrations Commence Across GTA Online with Gifts from the Happy Holidays Hauler, a Free Snowball Launcher, and More Plus, Get the New Bravado Dorado SUV, Double Rewards on Entourage (Festive Remix), and Enjoy a Stocking Full of Festive Items

December 21, 2023

Snow-filled skies and festive wonder abounds as the holiday season arrives in Southern San Andreas, with new Snowball Fights, a holiday spin on Entourage, and a state-wide hunt for the mysterious Yeti.

Seasonal publicity stunts are customary this time of year, but eCola has gone the extra mile by summoning its Happy Holidays Hauler truck from plumes of red and green smoke. Look out for its string-lit, cherry-red paint job and unique jingle as it makes the rounds to and from Legion Square before vanishing into thin air. The Happy Holidays Hauler will be dropping presents in its wake including festive sweaters, GTA$ and RP, ammo, and snacks to pad your inventory.

Get yourself the gifts that keep on giving — real estate and passive income. Invest in the new Salvage Yard featured in GTA Online: The Chop Shop and partake in a rotation of Salvage Yard Robberies with new targets and locations this week.

There’s also a sleigh’s worth of holiday-themed gifts and rewards available, including the Green Xmas Tree Hat, White Xmas Reindeer Hat, and much more. Anyone who didn’t get the Snowman Outfit or the Gooch Mask last year will have a second chance at these eccentric accessories, thanks to the return of a string of holiday events.

Plus, the new Bravado Dorado (SUV) hits the snow-filled streets, there are Double Rewards in the new Drift Races, 3X GTA$ and RP for taking part in a new batch of holiday-themed Community Series Jobs, and gifts aplenty that all point to a sweet beginning to the New Year.
